What is CTF?

CTF is a cybersecurity competition. Teams of 5 people solve as many tasks as possible in 24 hours.
Common types of tasks:

Web art of hacking websites
PWN art of binary exploitation
OSINT art of searching in the internet
Forensics art of investigating cybercrime
Steganography the art of hiding information
Cryptography art of decrypting ciphers
Reverse engineering art of reading binary code

What is A/D?

Attack-Defense is a subgenre of CTF. It's harder, but funnier.

In A/D every team has same several running services with vulnarabilities. Teams find vulnarabilities, fix them in their services and write exploits to hack other's services.

What is A/D trainer?

A/D trainer lets you play A/D without friends, teams, at any time and where you want.
If you want to prepare for an A/D competition, you are in the right place.
It's basically a simulation of a real game, only for you.
